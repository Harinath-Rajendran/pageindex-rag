About DocuMind

DocuMind
Version 2.0 · Enterprise Document Intelligence

Vectorless, reasoning-based document Q&A with workspace isolation and multi-provider LLM support. Supports Claude, OpenAI, Grok and Ollama backends. Upload PDF, DOCX, XLSX, CSV, TXT and MD files and get precise, cited answers across your workspace documents.
